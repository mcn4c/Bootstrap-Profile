About Me

I'm a former teacher from Charlottesville, Va embarking on a new career as a web developer. I taught Special Education in Albemarle County for five years before I moved to Prague to obtain TEFL certification. After gaining TEFL, I moved to South Korea where I taught English at kindergartens for four years. While there, I was able to travel around East Asia sight-seeing and eating delicious food. My favorite food in the world is Korean food. Since I've moved back stateside I have become interested in switching career paths and think web development is an interesting and exciting field.

In my spare time, I enjoy baking, reading, walking in the woods, and watching good television. Some pictures of my baking projects are on my Profile page as well as pictures I took while living abroad or walking in the woods.
